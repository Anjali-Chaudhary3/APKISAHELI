Aim

APKI SAHELI, dedicated to spreading awareness about the unique health challenges faced by rural women during puberty and beyond.

We believe that every woman deserves access to quality healthcare, regardless of where she lives. By raising awareness about the health challenges faced by rural women, we hope to make a positive impact on the lives of women and girls around the country.The long term goal is to spread this initiative to the entire world.

Thank you for visiting our website and joining us in this important mission.

The Origin Of "APKI SAHELI"

Our website is designed to be a comprehensive resource for women living in rural areas who may not have access to traditional healthcare services. We cover a range of topics, including menstrual health, sexual and reproductive health, and mental health. Our team of experts has carefully curated a collection of articles, videos, and other resources that are easy to understand and accessible to everyone.

In addition to our informative content, we also offer a community chat where women can connect with each other and with healthcare professionals to get answers to their questions and find support. Our community chat is moderated by trained volunteers who are passionate about helping women in rural areas get the care and support they need.

Articles on women's healthcare are to educate and inform women about various aspects of their health and well-being.

Videos provided are effective tool for healthcare education, as they can provide a visual demonstration to educate and empower women to take control of their health and well-being.

Multiple links of related articles and various schemes are available on the website

Community chat provide a powerful platform for connection, support, and empowerment.
